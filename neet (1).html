NEET Exam 2024: Complete Details

Overview

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is the only medical entrance exam in India for admissions to MBBS, BDS, and other undergraduate medical courses. It is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) and is mandatory for admission to medical colleges across the country.

What is the NEET Exam?

The NEET exam is a national-level medical entrance test required for students aspiring to pursue undergraduate courses in medicine, such as MBBS and BDS. NEET is held annually and is mandatory for admission to both government and private medical colleges across India.

NEET 2024: Latest Updates

The NEET 2024 exam will follow the same syllabus and exam pattern as previous years, focusing on 11th and 12th-grade topics in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. NTA has made minor updates to the online registration process, ensuring a smoother application experience for candidates.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students must have completed their 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Biotechnology.
  • General category candidates need a minimum of 50% in their 10+2 exams (40% for reserved categories).
  • Students must be at least 17 years old by the time of admission.

NEET Registration 2024: Step-by-Step Process

To register for NEET 2024, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official NEET website.
  2. Click on "New Registration" and provide your personal and academic details.
  3. Upload your recent photograph, signature, and other required documents.
  4. Pay the application fee online using a debit/credit card or net banking.
  5. Submit the application form and keep a copy for future reference.

Preparation Tips

  • Understand the NEET syllabus thoroughly, covering 11th and 12th-grade Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • Practice previous year papers to get familiar with the types of questions asked.
  • Join mock tests and time yourself to improve speed and accuracy.
  • Focus on NCERT textbooks for Biology, as they cover most of the exam's content.
  • Revise regularly and focus on strengthening weaker subjects.

Result and Cut-Off

The NEET 2024 results will be declared within a month after the exam. You can check the results on the official NEET website. The cut-off marks vary every year, and admission depends on your score, category, and the number of seats available in medical colleges.

How to Send NEET Scores to Colleges

NEET scores are automatically sent to medical colleges during the counselling process. Candidates must register for the central or state-level counselling and provide their college preferences. Admissions are based on rank, seat availability, and category.
