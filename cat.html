CAT Exam 2024: Everything You Need to Know

Overview

The Common Admission Test (CAT) is a computer-based test held in India. The test scores a candidate on the basis of Quantitative Ability (QA), Verbal Ability (VA), Reading Comprehension (RC), Data Interpretation (DI), and Logical Reasoning (LR). CAT is an entrance exam for admission into management programs at premier business schools like IIMs and other top MBA colleges in India.

What is the CAT Exam?

The CAT exam is one of the most prestigious entrance exams for business schools in India. It tests candidates on a variety of skills, including quantitative ability, verbal and reading comprehension, data interpretation, and logical reasoning. A good CAT score opens up the path to admission into top management institutions in India, including the IIMs and other reputed MBA colleges.

CAT 2024: Latest Updates

The CAT 2024 exam pattern remains consistent with previous years. The test is divided into three sections: Quantitative Ability, Data Interpretation & Logical Reasoning, and Verbal Ability & Reading Comprehension. The total exam duration is 2 hours, and the total marks for the exam is 300.

CAT Registration 2024: Step-by-Step Process

Follow the steps below to register for CAT 2024:

  1. Visit the official website of CAT.
  2. Create a new user by registering with your email ID and mobile number.
  3. Login using your registration credentials and fill in your personal, academic, and contact details.
  4. Upload required documents, including your photograph and signature.
  5. Pay the exam fee of INR 2,000 (INR 1,000 for reserved category candidates).
  6. Submit the application and print the confirmation for future reference.

Preparation Tips

  • Understand the exam pattern and syllabus thoroughly.
  • Focus on strengthening your basics in QA, DILR, and VARC.
  • Practice previous year question papers and attempt mock tests regularly.
  • Improve time management by solving timed practice sets.

Result and Cut-Off

The CAT 2024 results will be declared in January 2025. The result can be checked on the official CAT website. The cut-off scores vary for different management institutions and depend on the exam's difficulty level.

How to Send CAT Scores to Institutes

CAT scores can be sent to the participating institutions by filling in the relevant information during the registration process. Candidates can apply to multiple institutes based on their CAT score.
