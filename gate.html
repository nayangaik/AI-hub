GATE Exam 2024: Everything You Need to Know

Overview

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is an exam that primarily tests the comprehensive understanding of various undergraduate subjects in engineering and science for admission to postgraduate programs and securing jobs in public sector companies. GATE is conducted jointly by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) and seven Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) on behalf of the National Coordination Board (NCB)-GATE.

What is the GATE Exam?

The GATE exam tests the aptitude and subject knowledge of candidates seeking admission into postgraduate engineering and science programs or securing jobs in public sector enterprises (PSUs). GATE 2024 is conducted in a fully online mode across multiple sessions in February 2024. With a GATE score, candidates can apply for admissions in M.Tech., MS, or Ph.D. programs across top engineering institutes in India, including the IITs and IISc.

GATE 2024: Latest Updates

The GATE 2024 exam pattern has been updated. The paper will consist of multiple-choice questions (MCQs), multiple select questions (MSQs), and numerical answer type (NAT) questions. A new paper on Data Science and Artificial Intelligence has been added to the list of subjects. The total exam duration is 3 hours, and the total marks for the exam is 100.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree holders in Engineering/Technology (4 years after 10+2 or 3 years after Diploma in Engineering/Technology).
  • Students in their final year of Bachelor’s degree can also apply.
  • There is no age limit for the GATE exam.

GATE Registration 2024: Step-by-Step Process

Follow the steps below to register for GATE 2024:

  1. Visit the official website of GATE.
  2. Create a new user by registering with your email ID and mobile number.
  3. Login using your registration credentials and fill in your personal, academic, and contact details.
  4. Upload required documents, including your photograph and signature.
  5. Pay the exam fee of INR 1,500 (INR 750 for female and reserved category candidates).
  6. Submit the application and print the confirmation for future reference.

GATE Exam Centres

The GATE 2024 exam will be conducted in various cities across India and a few international locations. Major cities include:

  • Delhi
  • Mumbai
  • Kolkata
  • Bangalore
  • Chennai
  • Hyderabad

Preparation Tips

  • Start early and create a study plan.
  • Focus on understanding concepts rather than memorization.
  • Practice with previous years' GATE question papers.
  • Attempt mock tests to improve time management.
  • Revise regularly and work on weak areas.

Result and Cut-Off

The GATE 2024 results will be declared in March 2024. The result can be checked on the official GATE website. The cut-off scores vary for different branches of engineering and depend on the difficulty level of the exam.

How to Send GATE Scores to Universities

GATE scores can be sent to various institutions by entering the details during the application process. Additionally, candidates can opt to send their GATE scores to PSUs during the registration process.
